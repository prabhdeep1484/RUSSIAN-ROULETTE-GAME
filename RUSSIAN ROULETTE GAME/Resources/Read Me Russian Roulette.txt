Game Read ME

Points
1.Bullet fired in the shoot Away You get 100 Bonus Points
2.Bullet not fired on shoot Yourself you get 10 points.
3.If you reach in to the 6th or final round you get 100 Bonus Points

Player Instructions

1. Player Name is must to start the Game
2.Player have options to see the Highest Scores and other players scores by clicking on scores.

Game Working

Load Gun-->ShootAway or Shoot Yourself --> Die or Survive --> Going to Next Round -->Completing six Levels lead to Game Over --> Got Option to Restart Game

You get two chances to shoot away
You get 100 bonus points for firing bullet on shoot away chance
you get 10 points each for surviving on shoot yourself
